What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to models that generate new content after learning patterns from vast datasets.

  • Language models (LLMs): predict the next token to produce coherent text and code.
  • Diffusion models: iteratively denoise random pixels to create images from prompts.
  • Audio & speech models: synthesize natural-sounding voices and music.

Why it matters

GenAI accelerates ideation, automates routine work, and augments creativity. Teams ship faster while focusing on higher-value decisions.

How it Works

  3. Inference: generate responses conditioned on your prompt.

Prompting guidelines

  • State the goal and constraints up front.
  • Provide examples for style or format.
  • Ask for structure (tables, steps, JSON).
  • Iterate: refine with follow-ups.

Responsible AI

Principles

  • Transparency: disclose when content is AI-assisted.
  • Privacy: minimize personal data; obtain consent.
  • Safety: avoid harmful outputs; use guardrails.
  • Accountability: keep a human in the loop.
  • Attribution: respect IP and cite sources.

Evaluation checklist

  • Clear user value and success metrics.
  • Mitigations for bias, toxicity, hallucinations.
  • Security reviews for prompt injection.
  • Fallbacks and safe-failure behaviors.
